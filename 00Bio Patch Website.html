About BioPatch

BioPatch builds simple, trustworthy tools to help people stay ahead of their health. Our weekly patch reads multiple biomarkers non-invasively and pairs with a mobile app to give clear, personalized recommendations. We design for privacy, accessibility, and clinical reliability.

  • Medical-grade validated sensors and algorithms
  • Simple apply once a week, painless
  • Private data encryption & opt-in sharing
  • Designed for everyday users — athletes, seniors, and busy people

The BioPatch

Ultra-thin, flexible patch with discreet sensors. Designed for comfort and one-week wear. Biocompatible adhesive keeps it stable during daily life.

  • • Measures: heart rate variability, skin temperature, hydration proxy, motion/activity
  • • Battery: week-long passive operation
  • • Materials: breathable polymer, hypoallergenic adhesive
  • • Disposal: single-use recyclable sleeve
